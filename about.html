About

I started in Chișinău — applied informatics, then a master's in software — writing C#/.NET for logistics systems. The route since ran through Belgium, the Isle of Man, Birmingham, and the London market: pensions platforms at Delta Financial Systems, insurance comparison at Compare the Market, and now specialty insurance at AXA XL, where I lead Data & AI engineering and an AI Lab of 15+ engineers and data scientists. Along the way I picked up an MSc in Big Data Analytics and a habit I never lost: being the person who takes the proof-of-concept everyone demos and turns it into the production system everyone relies on.

The pattern across fourteen years is the same one twice a decade: a promising system meets messy reality — legacy migrations, event-driven rebuilds, and now AI systems that have to survive observability, audit, and actual users. That last mile is my specialty, and in financial services it's the mile that matters.

Off the clock: gym, swimming, reading, family, travel.
