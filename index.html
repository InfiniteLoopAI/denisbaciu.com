04 / About

I started in Chișinău — applied informatics, then a master's in software — writing C#/.NET for logistics systems. The route since ran through Belgium, the Isle of Man, Birmingham, and the London market: pensions platforms, insurance comparison, and now specialty insurance at AXA XL, where I lead Data & AI engineering and an AI Lab of 15+ engineers and data scientists.

The pattern across fourteen years is the same one twice a decade: a promising system meets messy reality. That last mile — observability, audit, actual users — is my specialty, and in financial services it's the mile that matters.
